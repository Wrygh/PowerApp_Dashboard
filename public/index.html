PowerApp Dashboard

POWERSTEP is an innovative IoT system designed for foot traffic monitoring and energy generation through footstep-powered LED lighting. Utilizing advanced piezoelectric technology, it captures the energy produced by footsteps to power LEDs, making it an eco-friendly solution for public spaces. By continuously monitoring foot traffic, POWERSTEP not only lights up pathways but also provides valuable insights into movement patterns, helping optimize space usage and enhance energy efficiency. This system transforms every step into a sustainable source of power while ensuring seamless real-time data transmission.
